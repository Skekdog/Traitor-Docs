title: Lighting, Gravity, and Materials
---

To change your map's lighting, adjust properties in the Lighting service in Explorer. You can additionally add instances, like Atmosphere, under that service.

To change your map's gravity, adjust the Gravity property in the Workspace service.

To add custom Materials, add MaterialVariants to the MaterialService service.

When uploading your map with the SDK, these will all automatically be bundled into the map. Do not attempt to manually add these to the map, as these will be overwritten.
