title: Lighting, Gravity, and Materials
---

The SDK will automatically upload your map with the properties you specify in Lighting, Workspace, and Terrain. Instances in these services (and in MaterialService) will also be uploaded.

When uploading your map with the SDK, these will all automatically be bundled into the map. Do not attempt to manually add these to the map, as these will be overwritten.
